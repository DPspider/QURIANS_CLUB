About Qurians Club

Qurians Club is the premier student-led initiative at Ajeenkya D. Y. Patil University, dedicated to building analytical, logical, and career-ready minds. We are a vibrant community of thinkers, problem-solvers, and future leaders, united by our passion for Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Placement Preparation.

  • Established: 14th October 2022
  • Location: Pune, Maharashtra
  • Active Members: 1000+ across Maharashtra
  • Focus Areas: Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning (QALR), Placement Prep
Qurians isn’t just a club—it’s a movement building minds that reason, solve, and succeed.
Our Vision

“To be a leading student-driven platform fostering analytical excellence and professional preparedness through experiential learning and structured aptitude training.”

Our Mission
  • Empower students with the skills and confidence needed to excel in competitive exams, campus placements, and real-world problem-solving.
  • Create a collaborative environment where learning is fun, practical, and impactful.
Core Objectives
  1. Foster QALR Culture: Promote analytical and logical thinking through contests, quizzes, and peer learning.
  2. Placement Readiness: Organize mock tests, resume reviews, group discussions, and aptitude sessions aligned with industry hiring standards.
  3. Competitive Exam Support: Prepare students for exams like CAT, GRE, GATE, UPSC, IBPS, and more.
  4. Super IQ Recognition: Identify and celebrate top performers with leaderboards, awards, and social media highlights.
  5. Bridge Theory & Practice: Develop decision-making under pressure, mental agility, and interview confidence.
  6. Collaborate & Create: Partner with other clubs, mentors, and corporate trainers for holistic growth.
Year-wise Evolution
  • AY 2022–23: Club launched, QALR 1.0 event held, committee formed, 100+ members joined in 2 months
  • AY 2023–24: Events: Buzz Off, Brain’s Tashan, new verticals (Marketing, Technical, Management), Super IQ Wall of Fame, first large-scale inter-department event
  • AY 2024–25: Integration with Placement Dept., Tech Titan’s Trivia, Tech Mahindra Drive, 25+ students selected via mock/training prep, committee expanded to 20+ leads
  • AY 2025–26: Podcast: Campus to Corporate, TCS Drive Mock Aptitude, LinkedIn Masterclass, #Tech 2025 Drama, Apti Quest, diversity hiring, public speaking, 300+ members, Qurians in placement reports
